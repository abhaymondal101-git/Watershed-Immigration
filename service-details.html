Express Entry

Express Entry is one of the most popular and probably the fastest way to get permanent residence in Canada. It is the easiest way for immigrants to start living their life in Canada, along with the multitude of benefits that Canada has to offer.

It is an online procedure, which was implemented by the Canadian Government to process the applications of skilled workers who are willing to immigrate to Canada, work here, and get their Permanent Resident (PR) Card from Canada. This is the only system is programmed to manage Federal Programs – FSW (Federal Skilled Worker), CEC (Canadian Experience Class), and FST (Federal Skilled Traders).
